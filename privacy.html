Privacy Policy

R&B Ganderbal Application  ·  Last updated: 24 December 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application R&B Ganderbal, published on Google Play Store and developed and maintained by Neovant Advance Solutions Private Limited ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"). It explains how information is collected, used, stored, protected, and deleted in relation to this application.

01Purpose of the Application

The R&B Ganderbal application is developed to support official Government operations, including project monitoring, progress reporting, administrative coordination, and verification of field-level activities.

02Information Collected

The following categories of information may be collected during use of the application:

  • Full name and official contact details
  • Designation, role, and access permissions
  • Authentication credentials and session identifiers
  • Location data (only during active use and with explicit consent)
  • Images or documents uploaded for official purposes
  • Device information and diagnostic logs

03Use of Information

Information is used strictly for legitimate Government and operational purposes, including authentication, authorisation, activity verification, system auditing, and service improvement. Data is never used for commercial profiling or advertising.

04Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or commercially exploit user data. Access is strictly limited to authorised Government officials and designated technical personnel only. Data is not shared with third parties except where required by law or official directive.

05Data Storage and Security

Appropriate technical and organisational safeguards are implemented to protect your data against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission over secure connections
  • Secure, access-controlled server infrastructure
  • Role-based access controls limiting data visibility
  • Regular security reviews and audit logging

06Application Permissions

The application requests the following device permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

Location
Field activity verification and geotagging of official site visits
Camera
Capturing official site photographs and documentation
Storage
Uploading and accessing official documents and media

Permissions are requested only when necessary and can be revoked at any time via your device settings without affecting non-dependent features.

07Children's Privacy

This application is intended exclusively for authorised Government personnel and is not designed for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such collection is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

08Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in operational practice, legal requirements, or application functionality. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the application following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

09Account Deletion

Users of the R&B Ganderbal application may request permanent deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

To submit a deletion request, email us at info@neovant.in with the subject line: Account Deletion – R&B Ganderbal

For full details on what data is deleted, retention obligations, and processing timeframes, see our Account Deletion page.
