Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Neovant Advanced Solutions (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information in relation to mobile and web applications developed and maintained for use by Government departments and associated agencies.

The applications covered under this policy are intended exclusively for official and authorized use and are not designed as consumer-facing public services.

1. Purpose of the Application

The application is developed to support Government operations including, but not limited to, project monitoring, progress reporting, administrative coordination, and verification of field-level activities.

2. Information Collected

Depending on authorized usage, the application may collect the following categories of information:

  • Full name, official email address, and contact number
  • Designation, role, and access permissions
  • Authentication credentials and session identifiers
  • Location data, collected only during active usage and with consent
  • Images or media uploaded as part of official documentation
  • Device information and application diagnostic logs

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used strictly for legitimate operational purposes, including user authentication, authorization, project tracking, activity verification, system auditing, and service improvement.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Company does not sell, rent, or commercially exploit any user data. Access to information is limited to authorized Government officials and designated technical personnel solely for maintenance, support, or compliance purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are implemented to protect information, including encrypted data transmission, secure server environments, and role-based access controls.

6. Application Permissions

  • Location access – to tag and verify official field updates
  • Camera access – to capture work-related images
  • Storage access – to upload official documents or images

Permissions are requested only when required and may be revoked by the user through device settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

The application is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. The Company does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in legal, regulatory, or Government requirements. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
