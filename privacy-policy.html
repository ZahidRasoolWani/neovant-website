Privacy Policy – R&B Ganderbal

Last updated: 24 December 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application “R&B Ganderbal” (“the App”), published on Google Play Store and developed and maintained by Neovant Advanced Solutions Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

This policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, protected, and deleted in relation to the R&B Ganderbal mobile application.

1. Purpose of the Application

The R&B Ganderbal application is developed to support official Government operations, including project monitoring, progress reporting, administrative coordination, and verification of field-level activities.

2. Information Collected

  • Full name and official contact details

Information is used strictly for legitimate Government and operational purposes, including authentication, authorization, activity verification, system auditing, and service improvement.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or commercially exploit user data. Access is limited to authorized Government officials and designated technical personnel only.

5. Data Storage and Security

Appropriate technical and organizational safeguards are implemented, including encrypted transmission, secure servers, and role-based access control.

6. Application Permissions

  • Location: Field activity verification
  • Camera: Capturing official images

Permissions are requested only when necessary and can be revoked via device settings.

The application is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
