Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Neovant Advanced Solutions (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information through applications developed for Government agencies.

1. Purpose of the Application

The mobile application is developed exclusively for official use by authorized personnel of a Government department for project monitoring, reporting, and administrative purposes.

2. Information Collected

  • Name, official email address, phone number
  • User role, designation, and project access
  • Authentication and session identifiers
  • Location data (only while using the app and with permission)
  • Images uploaded for official project documentation
  • Device and diagnostic information

3. Use of Information

Information is used strictly for authentication, project tracking, verification of field work, system security, and operational purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or commercialize data. Access is limited to authorized Government officials and designated technical administrators for maintenance and support.

5. Data Security

Industry-standard security measures including encrypted transmission and access control are implemented to safeguard information.

6. Permissions

  • Location – for tagging official work updates
  • Camera – for capturing work images
  • Storage – for uploading images

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to comply with legal, regulatory, or Government requirements. Updates will be published on this page.
