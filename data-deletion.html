Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 24 December 2025

Neovant Advanced Solutions develops and maintains software applications for official government and enterprise use. We respect user privacy and provide a formal process for requesting deletion of personal data associated with these applications.

How to Request Deletion of Your Data

If you are an authorized user of an application developed by Neovant Advanced Solutions and wish to request deletion of your personal data, please submit a request by email:

email

Contact Email

info@neovant.in

Please include the following information to help us verify your request:

  • Registered email address or username
  • Name of the application
  • Government department or organization
  • Type of request: Data deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification, we may delete the following data, where applicable:

  • User profile details (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data collected during authorized app usage
  • Uploaded images, documents, and related media
  • Associated project activity and operational records

Data Retention and Legal Obligations

info

Certain data may be retained where required by applicable laws, regulatory requirements, audit obligations, or directives issued by the relevant government authority. Any retained data is securely stored and access-restricted.

Processing Timeframe

schedule

Verified data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 working days, subject to legal and administrative requirements.
