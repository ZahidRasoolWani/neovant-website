Account & Data
Deletion Request

R&B Ganderbal Application  ·  Last updated: 24 December 2025

This page applies specifically to the Android application R&B Ganderbal, published on Google Play Store and developed and maintained by Neovant Advance Solutions Private Limited. Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data using the process described below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and personal data associated with the R&B Ganderbal application, send an email to:

Contact Email info@neovant.in

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Registered email address or username used in the application
  • Application name: R&B Ganderbal
  • Government department or organisation (if applicable)
  • Request type: Account Deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of your request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account and profile information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Location data collected during app usage
  • Uploaded images, documents, and related media
  • Associated activity and operational records

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, Government regulations, audit obligations, or official directives. Any retained data is securely stored and access-restricted in compliance with applicable data protection standards.

Processing Timeframe

Verified account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 working days, subject to legal and administrative requirements.

You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been received and again once deletion is complete.
